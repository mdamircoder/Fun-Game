You and Your Friend are playing a game : OlympicGame_1
------------------------------------------------------
Both of you start from 50. Use a coin (Head/Tails).
-----------------------------------------------------
Who can reach 100 first ? You or Your Friend ?
-----------------------------------------------------
You go +2 if it is Head and -1 if it is Tails.
If you reach 100, you win !
If you reach 0, you are out, lose !
-----------------------------------------------------
You may quit the game any time you want, it will show
you your current level.
-----------------------------------------------------
The COIN toss is done by system, a random event.
-----------------------------------------------------
Best Of Luck. Let's Play !
